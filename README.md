## RPC Sun Project – Calculator on Solaris
<br>
This project is a TCP-based calculator program developed on Solaris, offering 10 operations:

- Addition of two floats
- Subtraction of two floats
- Multiplication of two floats
- Division of two floats (divisor ≠ 0)
- Modulo of two floats
- Exponentiation (float1 to the power of float2)
- Maximum of two floats
- Minimum of two floats
- Square root of a float
- Factorial of a float
The program also includes an option to exit by selecting 0.
